## Position Operator

$$
\hat{x}|\psi\rangle = x|\psi\rangle
$$

## Momentum Operator

$$
\hat{p} = -i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial x}
$$

## Hamiltonian (Energy)

$$
\hat{H} = \frac{\hat{p}^2}{2m} + V(\hat{x})
$$

## Commutator

$$
[\hat{x}, \hat{p}] = i\hbar
$$
