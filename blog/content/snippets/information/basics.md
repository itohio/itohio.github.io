## Information Content

$$
I(x) = -\log_2 p(x)
$$

Rare events carry more information.

## Key Concepts

- **Entropy**: Average information content
- **Mutual Information**: Shared information between variables
- **Channel Capacity**: Maximum reliable transmission rate
